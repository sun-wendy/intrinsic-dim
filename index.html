Research Question

Our research explores the geometric properties of features learned by image models. Through activation visualization and analysis of key metrics, particularly the intrinsic dimensionality of feature spaces, we seek to understand the structure of feature manifolds and their interrelationships. This investigation aims to illuminate how neural networks represent and transform visual information across their layers.
